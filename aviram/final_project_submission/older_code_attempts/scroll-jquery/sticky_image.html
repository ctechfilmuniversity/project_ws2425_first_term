#RunBlue Africa

Mina Guli is running 200 marathons to draw attention to the global water crisis.

The #RunBlue teams ran with Mina Guli in these African countries.

#RunBlue Kenya at Lake Naivasha highlighted the interdependent role of the lake in agriculture, horticulture and all life surrounding the lake.

#RunBlue Tanzania's #RunningWaterHeroes ran with @MinaGuli.

#RunBlue Zambia at @WWFZambia ran for conservation: "We ran for the rivers that provide life and livelihoods for wildlife, nature and people".

#RunBlue Malawi - "Water is life, but trees are also life - where there are trees, there is water", explained Frackson Kachiwawa, at Dzalanyama Forest Lodge in Malawi.

#RunBlue Zimbabwe - "Water is everything. It’s time we stopped treating it as if it’s nothing."" #MosiOaTunya.

#RunBlue South Africa: Mina completed her #RunBlue Marathon 67 in South Africa
